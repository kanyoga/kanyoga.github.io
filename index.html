Who is Kandiah Yogamoorthy

GREATEST CHARACTERISTIC

Conformity is your strongest characteristic. 50 to 70 percent of your personality is based upon this trait; most things you do will be influenced by this characteristic more than any other.

You are fair and have a sense of decency. You are likely to be angered by things which are unjust, unfair or indecent. You are loyal to your loved ones, friends, and acquaintances. You have a strong sense of “right and wrong”.

You enjoy making lists and itemizing things so that you can keep priorities in order. You like to double-check things to make sure they are right. You prefer things to be precise, with attention to detail. You hate things that are “helter-skelter”. You like proven methods; “tried and true” makes a lot of sense to you.

If it is necessary to begin a project of any kind, you like to know exactly how to do it, how much time it will take and how much it will cost. If you feel you can’t do it right, you’d rather not do it. This means you are not likely to make mistakes if you are thoroughly acquainted with what you are doing.

You are keenly aware of the future and what it holds for you. You have an appreciation of tradition. This means you will likely dislike constant or sudden changes in your life. You don’t like taking chances or risks.

SECONDARY CHARACTERISTIC

Internalist is your secondary characteristic. This will guide your personality in areas complimentary to your strongest characteristic, and will strongly influence your actions in day-to-day life.

You like serious conversation, and do not care much for triviality. You like to use your imagination, and can come up with ideas that, while at first seem unusual, often have merit. You prefer to have a few close friends rather than a number of general acquaintances. You enjoy earnest, truthful relationships. You enjoy privacy. You like to contemplate your actions and reactions to the world around you.

LEADERSHIP METHOD

You will expect from your people precision, accuracy, and results. You will be open to suggestion, if it is sensible.

PERSONALITY CHARACTERISTICS

You have a strong sense of duty. You can be counted on to keep your word, and to perform honorably, as long as you respect those in leadership positions. Once you accept a task, you commit yourself to its completion. You like knowing what is required of you, and working within an established order.

You are confident in your abilities. You are capable of making decision and weighing one thing against another. You enjoy heading a project, being in charge and organizing it to reach your goal. You tend to feel assured of success in any project you undertake. You are bold in setting your goals. You are able to form mental pictures, to create out of the process of mind alone.

You are pleasantly disposed, good-natured, and agreeable. You fellow patterns, regulated either by yourself or others. You like to live and act in a paced manner. You prefer to “live and let live”, not trying to enforce your beliefs on others. You are gentle and kind in disposition. You are moderate in habits and thoughts.

LIFESTYLE CHARACTERISTICS

You tend to be very cautious. You are conservative in nature, and sensitive to criticism.

You are loyal and conscientious. With you, quality comes first. You are perfectionistic; it is important to you that work be done right.

You prefer to tell rather than sell. You are analytical, creative and authoritative. This lends itself to decision-making processes … IF you have all the facts.

You like to be the “head” and be in charge of things, but you are patient. You take the time to listen to others and to do things right.

You enjoy the concept of respect. You like to have time alone to think and be creative. You like freedom from constant noise and external pressures.
